# Bioluminescence

Some living things make their own light through a chemical reaction.

## Where it happens

Bioluminescence is most common in the **deep ocean**, where sunlight never
reaches. Fireflies and some fungi glow on land, but the ocean is where it is
everywhere.

## Why it happens

Animals use their glow to find food, to attract mates, and to startle or
distract predators.
