# Photosynthesis

Plants convert sunlight into energy through a process called photosynthesis.

## How it works

Inside each leaf, tiny structures called chloroplasts capture light energy. They
use this energy to split water molecules and combine the hydrogen with carbon
dioxide from the air, producing glucose and releasing oxygen as a byproduct.

## Why it matters

Photosynthesis is the foundation of almost every food chain on Earth. It removes
carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and replaces it with the oxygen that animals
— including us — breathe.
